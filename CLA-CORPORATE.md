### 2. Patent License

Your company grants the project owner a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free, irrevocable (except as stated below) patent license to make, have made, use, offer to sell, sell, import, and otherwise transfer your employees' contributions. If your company files a patent claim against the project or any contributor relating to these contributions, this license terminates immediately.

### 3. Right to Relicense

Your company understands that the project may be relicensed (including to a proprietary license) in the context of an acquisition or business transaction. Your employees' contributions may be distributed under that new license.

## Representations

- Your company is legally entitled to grant the above licenses.
- Your employees' contributions are their original work made within the scope of their employment.
- To the best of your company's knowledge, the contributions do not infringe on any third-party rights.
- Your company has the authority to bind the entity to this agreement.

## How to Sign

Email **cla@agent-nervous-system.com** with:
1. Company legal name
2. List of authorized contributor GitHub usernames
3. Name and title of the signatory

The project maintainers will verify and record the agreement. Once signed, employees on the authorized list can contribute without individual CLA signing.
